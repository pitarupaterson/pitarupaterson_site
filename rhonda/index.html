Super powers...
For people who love drawing.

  • Rhonda gives you the ability to draw in 3D.
  • Rhonda strives to bring the immediacy of drawing with a pen & paper to a ground-breaking new environment.
  • Rhonda lets you draw your way in and out of space in a way that makes each drawing feel like its own little universe.
  • Rhonda frees you from the clutter of UI and palettes and brushes that you've unfortunately grown accustomed to.
  • Rhonda focuses your attention into a handful of breakthrough features that work seamlessly together to extend the natural process of drawing.
  • Rhonda is periodically updated to take advantage of new technology, bringing the essence of drawing to bleeding edge hardware.
  • Rhonda is mind-blowingly fun!

Pitaru Paterson
