Super powers,
for people who love drawing.

Our ongoing goal with Rhonda is to take the best of pen & paper, and the best of technology, and smash them together.

  • Rhonda gives you the ability to draw in 3D.
  • Rhonda strives to preserve the immediacy of pen & paper drawing, no matter how much technology is involved.
  • Rhonda frees you from the clutter of UI, palettes, and brushes that you've unfortunately grown accustomed to. Instead Rhonda focuses your attention on a handful of powerful features that work together to extend the natural process of drawing.
  • Rhonda is periodically updated to take advantage of exciting new hardware. We have many fantasies about where Rhonda might end up, and are working to take her there one step at a time.
