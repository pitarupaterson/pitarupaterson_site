Super powers...
For people who love drawing.

  • Rhonda gives you the ability to draw in 3D.
  • Rhonda lets you draw your way in and out of space in a way that makes each drawing feel like its own little universe.
  • Rhonda strives to preserve the immediacy of drawing with a pen & paper.
  • Rhonda frees you from the clutter of UI, palettes, and brushes that you've unfortunately grown accustomed to. Instead Rhonda focuses your attention on only a handful of features that work together to extend the natural process of drawing.
  • Rhonda is periodically updated to take advantage of exciting new hardware. We have fantasies of where Rhonda might end up, and are working to take here there one step at a time.
  • Rhonda is mind-blowingly fun!
