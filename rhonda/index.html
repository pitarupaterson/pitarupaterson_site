Super powers,
for people who love drawing.

Our goal with Rhonda is to take the best of pen & paper, and the best of technology, then smash them together into the definitive 3D drawing app.

  • Rhonda strives to preserve the immediacy of pen & paper drawing.

  • Instead of a clutter of UI & palettes, Rhonda focuses your attention on a handful of features that work together to extend the natural process of drawing.

  • We periodically update Rhonda and adapt it to take advantage of exciting new hardware.
