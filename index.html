We're a product development group with one foot in Montreal and the other in Brooklyn. Most of our time is spent trying to answer questions like:

  • If you were a counting robot with a gaggle of ghosts living in your torso, what would be your favorite song?
  • What's the best way to understand and address risk when growing your money?
  • Is there any better place to teach than the kitchen table?
  • How could you draw as freely in 3D space as you would with a ballpoint and a cheap piece of looseleaf?
  • What if we could sculpt sound as though we were throwing pottery?
  • Shouldn't there be a game that provides the same sort of tactile reward as popping a great zit?
  • Wouldn't it be wonderful if there was an easy way to send someone a handwritten email?
  • Could the process of creating animation ever be as immediate as playing a harmonica? (Sorry iOS, it's flash)
  • What do choreography & programming?
  • Can you bottle childlike delight? (Sorry iOS, it's flash)
  • What's the ultimate paper airplane design?
  • What does it feel like right before you launch?

If you have any thoughts you'd like to share, or ideas about how we might work together, get in touch.
