Pitaru Paterson

James Paterson and Amit Pitaru have been collaborating on art and commercial project for over a decade.

Their early experimental projects can be seen on InsertSilence.
Later on, they co-developed the 3D drawing tool Rhonda.
Very recently they have released a tiny iPad app about a counting Robot, that they hope will one day take over the world.

They are also working on commissioned projects for a selected number of clients. You can engage them as Creative Directors that think in code, or seasoned developers with a creative spirit.
